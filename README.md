# Supermarket-sales-dashboard
## Supermarket Sales Dashboard (Excel)

This repository contains a comprehensive **Supermarket Sales Dashboard** built in Microsoft Excel. It provides high-level KPIs and granular visualizations for sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior for the year 2023.

### Key Features & KPIs

* **Total Sales:** $427,998
* **Total Profit:** $26,624
* **Profit Margin:** 107%
* **Top Product:** Men's Leather Jacket ($27,763 in sales; 186 units)
* **Top Category:** Toys & Gaming ($98,328 in sales)

### Visualizations

* **Temporal Analysis:** * **Monthly Trend:** Combined bar/line chart tracking Sales, Profit, and Profit % across the fiscal year.
* **Daily Sales:** Area chart showing daily fluctuations and peaks (1st through 31st).


* **Product & Category Performance:**
* **Product Leaderboard:** Horizontal bar chart comparing specific items like Electric Scooters, Office Chairs, and Recliner Sofas.
* **Category Treemap:** Visual breakdown of revenue by segment (Toys & Gaming, Fashion, Home & Furniture, etc.).


* **Sales Distribution:**
* **Sales Type (Pie Chart):** Breakdown by Direct Sales (37%), Online (32%), and Wholesaler (31%).
* **Payment Mode (Pie Chart):** Comparison of Online (71%) vs. Cash (29%) transactions.



### Interactivity (Slicers)

The dashboard includes dynamic filtering via the left and top sidebars:

* **Time Period:** Filter by Year (2023) and specific Month (Jan–Dec).
* **Transaction Details:** Filter by Sale Type (Direct, Online, Wholesaler) and Payment Mode (Cash, Online).

### Tech Stack

* **Microsoft Excel:** Pivot Tables, Power Query, Slicers, and Advanced Charting.
